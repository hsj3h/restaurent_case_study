# 🍽️ Restaurant Financial Model (Excel-Based)

## 📌 Overview

This project is a **comprehensive financial model for a restaurant business**, built using Excel. It covers end-to-end financial planning including revenue forecasting, cost estimation, profitability analysis, and cash flow management.

The model is designed for:

* Business planning
* Investment analysis
* Financial forecasting
* Startup feasibility evaluation

---

## 🧱 Model Structure

### 1. Assumptions Sheet

* Construction timeline (3 months)
* Operational start date
* Pricing (APC – Average Price per Customer)
* Customer volume (weekdays vs weekends)
* Growth assumptions (YoY & MoM)
* Occupancy and capacity planning

---

### 2. Revenue Model

* Segmented into:

  * **Lunch & Dinner**
  * **Weekdays & Weekends**
* Revenue streams:

  * Food
  * Alcoholic beverages
  * Non-alcoholic beverages
* Monthly revenue projections based on:

  * Footfall
  * Pricing
  * Growth trends

---

## 💰 Cost Structure

### 🔹 Direct Expenses

* Food cost (% of revenue)
* Alcoholic beverage cost
* Non-alcoholic beverage cost
* Staff salaries (operations team)

### 🔹 Indirect Expenses

* Rent (% of revenue)
* Royalty
* Utilities (water, electricity)
* Marketing
* Maintenance
* Admin salaries

---

## 📊 Profit & Loss Statement (P&L)

### Monthly P&L

* Revenue breakdown
* Direct & indirect costs
* EBITDA
* EBITDA margin %

### Annual P&L (3 Years)

* Revenue growth projection
* Expense scaling
* Profitability trends
* Net Profit Margin up to **~32% by Year 3**

---

## 🏗️ Capital Structure

* Total investment required: **₹225 Lakhs**
* Allocation:

  * Fixed Assets (Kitchen, Furniture, Decor)
  * Working Capital
  * Rent Deposit
* Funding:

  * 100% Equity

---

## 🧾 Fixed Asset Schedule

* Asset categories:

  * Kitchen equipment
  * Refrigeration
  * Furniture & fixtures
  * Restaurant décor
* Annual depreciation applied
* Closing balances tracked for 3 years

---

## 🔄 Working Capital Analysis

* Inventory (food & beverages)
* Payables (creditors)
* Net working capital requirement
* Yearly changes tracked

---

## 💵 Cash Flow Statement

* Operating cash flow
* Investing activities (capex)
* Financing activities (equity funding)
* Strong cash position growth over 3 years

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Strong revenue growth across 3 years
* EBITDA improves consistently
* Positive cash flow from Year 1
* Scalable and structured cost model
* No reliance on debt financing
